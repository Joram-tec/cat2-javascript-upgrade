About Us

Elpizo Eateries is a local food spot situated along Savannah Road in Donholm, Nairobi. We serve affordable, filling, and tasty Kenyan meals to the community — students, workers, and families alike.

Our kitchen opens early in the morning for breakfast and runs through to supper. Everything is cooked fresh and served with love.
