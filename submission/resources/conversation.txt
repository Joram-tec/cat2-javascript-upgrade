- I interviewed the owner of Elpizo Eateries, a man named Paul Onsongo, a local food spot situated along 
Savannah Road in Donholm, Nairobi.
- The main customers for is business are the local community — students, workers and families looking for affordable meals.
- The owner preferred blue and red colors to match their shop signage.
- The website should highlight the menu with prices, location and contact information so customers 
can easily find them and know what is available before visiting and after visiting.
